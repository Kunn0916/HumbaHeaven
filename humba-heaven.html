Our Story

Born & Raised
in Cebu

Humba Heaven was born from a simple love for Cebuano home cooking — the kind that fills a room with warm aroma and brings families together around a shared pot. Our founder, inspired by decades-old family recipes passed down through generations in Cebu City, opened Humba Heaven to share the soul of Cebuano cuisine with the world.


We believe great food starts with great ingredients. Every batch of humba is slow-braised for hours using native pork, fresh aromatics, and the perfect balance of soy, vinegar, and sweetness that makes Cebuano cooking unmistakable. No shortcuts. Just pure, honest flavor.
